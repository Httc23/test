    Hardware Agents

    Hon-Tsai Technology Co., Ltd. recognizes the importance of the security industry and is dedicated to developing security management systems for industries such as petrochemicals, fire safety, defense, manufacturing, and more. Our research and development team has successfully commercialized products in the 3K industries (hard, dirty, and dangerous jobs). These products are based on the same technology, revolving around the same concept, which combines software, mobile devices (such as PDAs, tablets, and smartphones), wireless communication technologies (such as RFID, NFC, 4G/5G, Wi-Fi, GPS), and the concepts of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and the Internet of Things (IoT).

    We provide security management solutions that use RFID/NFC or GPS with handheld mobile devices (such as PDAs, tablets, and smartphones) to assist businesses with inspection operations, occupational safety and quality audits, and contractor management. Since these operations are round-the-clock, especially outdoor inspection projects, information systems must be designed to allow operators to use mobile devices conveniently, even in harsh environmental conditions. Due to the need to integrate maintenance operations into the process, which may involve climbing, descending, and working in challenging environments such as high altitudes and waterways, the durability of inspection devices must be considered to prevent inaccurate inspections due to device failure. Factors such as font size, glare, vibration, rain, dust, drop resistance, offline operation, continuous usage time, and charging must be considered. Our company accumulates valuable experience from every planning project we participate in, and we only choose hardware agents that can withstand harsh environmental conditions, such as explosion-proof zones (Zone 1, Zone 2), IP65 dust and water resistance to prevent dust intrusion, and the ability to operate normally even in rainy weather. These devices are designed with a 1.8-meter drop resistance to prevent screen or casing damage in the event of a fall, and they pass military standard MIL-STD-810G certification, ensuring they can operate even when dropped on concrete surfaces or in harsh conditions like high temperatures, cold temperatures, heavy dust, and rain.
